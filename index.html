Degnan Labs Marine Genomics

Evolutionary and ecological genomics of marine invertebrate animals.

Research Focus

The research groups of Sandie and Bernie Degnan have diverse interest in ecological and evolutionary developmental biology and genomics of marine invertebrates

We're based at the University of Queensland in Brisbane, Australia, with easy access to the incredible biodiversity of the Great Barrier Reef, and the reefs of Moreton Bay, where we do most of our field work.
